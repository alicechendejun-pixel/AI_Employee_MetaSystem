# Hong Kong Market Rules Source Baseline

Use these as primary starting points and verify the latest page or circular before making a current claim.

## HKEX Trading Mechanism

Source:
`https://www.hkex.com.hk/Services/Trading/Securities/Overview/Trading-Mechanism?sc_lang=en`

Key items to verify:

- HKEX securities trading is order-driven.
- Pre-opening and Closing Auction Sessions accept auction order types.
- Continuous Trading Session supports limit, enhanced limit and special limit orders.
- Closing Auction Session securities use reference-price controls, including a first-stage range around the reference price.
- The Volatility Control Mechanism applies only to specified securities and uses instrument-specific trigger thresholds.
- Board-lot and odd-lot transactions have different execution characteristics.

## HKEX Settlement

Source:
`https://www.hkex.com.hk/Services/Settlement-and-Depository/Settlement?sc_lang=en`

Key item:

- Ordinary Hong Kong Exchange Trades and Clearing Agency Transactions are settled on T+2 through CCASS. China Connect securities have separate settlement arrangements.

## Stock Connect

Source:
`https://www.hkex.com.hk/Mutual-Market/Stock-Connect?sc_lang=en`

Check:

- Current eligible securities.
- Southbound trading days and sessions.
- Current investor and participant information books.
- Trading and settlement calendars.
- Quotas and current programme rules.

## Hong Kong Stock Stamp Duty

Source:
`https://www.gov.hk/en/residents/taxes/stamp/stamp_duty_rates.htm`

As of the Hong Kong Government page revised in May 2026, contract notes for both purchase and sale of Hong Kong stock are charged at 0.1% of consideration or value. Always verify the current rate and exemptions before calculating trading returns.

## Company Disclosures

Use HKEXnews for announcements, annual and interim reports, circulars, connected transactions, placements, rights issues, buybacks, suspensions and privatisation documents:

`https://www.hkexnews.hk/`

## Verification Rule

Do not rely on this reference file alone for current decisions. Confirm the live HKEX or Hong Kong Government page, record the access date and identify any instrument-specific exception.
